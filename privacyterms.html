Privacy Policy for MoxyTok

Last Updated: August 28, 2024

1. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will post any changes to this page and update the "Last Updated" date accordingly. Please review this policy regularly to stay informed of any changes.

2. Data Collection from KOLs

We collect personal and professional information from KOLs (Key Opinion Leaders) to facilitate collaborations, business partnerships, and marketing campaigns. This may include:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number, and business-related information (e.g., TikTok username, social media handles).
  • Professional information: Social media engagement metrics, follower demographics, content preferences, and previous collaboration history.
  • Communication records: Details from your communications with us regarding potential collaborations, sponsorships, or business inquiries.

3. Marketing Communications to KOLs

We send emails to KOLs for business purposes, including:

  • Collaboration proposals.
  • Sponsorship opportunities.
  • Updates on products or campaigns relevant to your audience.

You may opt out of these communications at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email or by contacting us directly at tiktokshop@moxytok.com.

4. Commission Rates and Paid Collaborations

In some of our communications, we may include details regarding commission rates or compensation terms as part of our collaboration offers. Any commissions, payment structures, or incentives discussed will be fully disclosed in the email. These offers are intended for business partnerships and do not imply any obligation unless a formal agreement is reached between both parties.

Some of our emails may contain affiliate or referral links, which are used to track the performance of marketing campaigns and collaboration opportunities. By clicking on these links, you acknowledge that MoxyTok may receive a commission or other financial benefits if you complete a transaction through these links. These links will be clearly labeled, and we will disclose any affiliate relationships in our communications.

6. Non-Binding Offers

Any commission rates, collaboration terms, or offers mentioned in our emails are for discussion purposes only and do not constitute a binding agreement. Formal collaboration agreements, including commission structures, must be finalized in a written contract signed by both parties before any work begins or payment is issued.
